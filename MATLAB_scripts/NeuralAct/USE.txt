NeuralAct
A matlab tool to visualize cortical activity on a three-dimensional model of the cortex
Author: Jan Kubanek (kubanek@stanford.edu)
Initially developed in 2005 at the Wadsworth Center in Albany, NY

If you find NeuralAct useful in your research, please refer to it as:
Kubanek J, Schalk G. NeuralAct: A Tool to Visualize Electrocortical (ECoG) Activity on a Three-Dimensional Model of the Cortex. Neuroinformatics (2015), 13, 167-74.
